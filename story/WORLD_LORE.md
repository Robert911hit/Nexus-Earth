Overview

Nexus Earth takes place in the year 2095.

The world is divided between Sovereign Cities and Fractured Zones.

The Sovereign Cities are powerful self-governing city states that emerged after the collapse of traditional nation governments.

The Fractured Zones exist outside city control and are home to independent settlements, traders, explorers, and free communities.

Historical Periods

The Unraveling

Climate disasters, resource conflicts, and economic instability weakened governments across the world.

The Consolidation

Megacorporations and regional powers established Sovereign Cities.

The Nexus Accord formally recognized these cities as the primary governing powers.

The Polished Age

Technology, infrastructure, and urban development expanded rapidly.

Life improved for many citizens inside Sovereign Cities.

Present Day

Tensions between control and freedom continue to grow.

New opportunities and new conflicts emerge throughout Nexus Earth.
