Robert Hit Dev

Title

The First Unregistered

Status

Missing

Overview

Robert Hit Dev was the first person to publicly reject corporate citizenship after the Nexus Accord.

At a time when every person belonged to a Sovereign City, Robert declared that human freedom should not require permission from governments, corporations, or systems.

He published a document known as the Unregistered Manifesto and disappeared shortly afterward.

Legacy

His ideas spread throughout Nexus Earth.

Many independent settlements, underground trade networks, and free communities trace their origins to his philosophy.

To some people he is a hero.

To others he is a dangerous symbol.

Mystery

The official record claims Robert Hit Dev disappeared voluntarily.

Many believe that story is false.

Rumors suggest he may still be alive somewhere within Nexus Earth.

No proof has ever been found.

Impact On Players

Every player begins the game as Unregistered.

Whether they realize it or not, they are walking a path first opened by Robert Hit Dev.
