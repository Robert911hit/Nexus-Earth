## BUS-001: Orin Transit
- **Name:** Orin Transit
- **Type:** Freight and transit
- **Owner:** Sable Orin
- **District:** The Fringe, Solenne
- **Description:** Orin Transit serves travelers, Auxiliary workers, Unregistered residents, and contractors operating near the border between Solenne and the Fractured Zones.
- **Services:** Local trade, contract referrals, repairs or documentation support, message drops, and limited credit depending on reputation.
- **Connections:** Maintains practical ties with The Fringe community, Gray Line intermediaries, and Solenne Integrated Systems inspectors when survival requires cooperation.
- **Story Hooks:** A missing ledger, delayed shipment, pressured employee, or unusual request can pull the player into work connected to Cael, Sable Orin, Maren Solis, Ezra Voss, or Robert Hit Dev rumors.
