# The Manifesto

## Identity

Type:
Ideological Organization

Location:
The Fringe, Solenne

Founder Influence:
Robert Hit Dev


## Overview

The Manifesto is the most faithful continuation of Robert Hit Dev's Unregistered philosophy.

They believe independence from corporate systems is not simply a legal status but a way of life.


## Leadership

The organization rejects centralized leadership.

Decisions are made through community councils.

This protects their ideals but makes rapid action difficult.


## Beliefs

- Human dignity exists outside corporate citizenship
- Communities can survive without corporate control
- Knowledge should remain free


## Relationship With Robert Hit Dev

The Manifesto believes they preserve his original vision.

They maintain the largest archive of his writings.


## Relationship With Solenne

They practice non-cooperation.

They do not attack Solenne.

They simply refuse to accept corporate authority.


## Player Benefits

- Lore access
- Safe houses
- Community reputation
- Robert Hit Dev investigation paths


## Risks

Association increases Solenne surveillance.

Corporate opportunities become limited.
