# The Gray Line

## Identity

Type:
Logistics Network / Underground Organization

Location:
The Fringe

Leader:
Sable Orin and other Coordinators


## Overview

The Gray Line manages movement between official and unofficial worlds.

They transport goods, people, information, and opportunities.


## Leadership

Controlled by Coordinators.

Promotion is based on reliability and successful operations.


## Activities

- Smuggling
- Information brokerage
- Identity services
- Logistics
- Unofficial contracts


## Relationship With Robert Hit Dev

They do not worship his legacy.

However, historical Gray Line operations are connected to his disappearance.


## Relationship With Solenne

A secret balance exists.

Solenne publicly opposes them but privately benefits from their existence.


## Player Benefits

- Underground markets
- Better movement options
- High-paying contracts
- Regional contacts


## Risks

The Gray Line protects itself first.

Members can be abandoned if necessary.
