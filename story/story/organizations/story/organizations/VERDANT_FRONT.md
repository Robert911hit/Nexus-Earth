# The Verdant Front

## Identity

Type:
Environmental Resistance Movement

Location:
The Fringe and Fractured Zones


## Overview

The Verdant Front believes Solenne's perfect environmental image hides damage occurring outside city borders.


## Leadership

Two layers exist:

Public activists:
- Media
- Awareness campaigns
- Legal resistance

Hidden cells:
- Direct action
- Covert operations


## Beliefs

- Environmental truth must be exposed
- Corporate systems hide consequences
- Communities deserve protection


## Relationship With Robert Hit Dev

They believe Robert discovered information about corporate environmental damage.

They use parts of his legacy to support their cause.


## Relationship With Solenne

Openly hostile.

Solenne classifies them as a destabilizing threat.


## Player Benefits

- Hidden information
- Investigative missions
- Fractured Zone alliances


## Risks

Their radical cells can act without warning.
