# Nexus Earth Settlement Database

Status: Supporting settlement database. No new Sovereign Cities or factions are created.

## SET-001: Fringe Settlement Node 01
- **Population:** 117 permanent residents plus travelers
- **Economy:** Repairs, food stalls, battery exchange, salvage, courier work, and informal lodging.
- **Description:** Fringe Settlement Node 01 is a small border community influenced by Unregistered ideas and dependent on practical trade with The Fringe.
- **Threats:** Supply shortages, Solenne inspection pressure, predatory brokers, route failures, and Fractured Zone weather damage.
- **Important NPCs:** Cael contacts, Ezra Voss archive runners, Gray Line couriers, local medics, and settlement elders.
