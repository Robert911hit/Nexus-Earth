# Sable Orin

## Identity

Name:
Sable Orin

Age:
42

Occupation:
Gray Line Coordinator

Location:
The Fringe, Solenne

Faction:
The Gray Line


## Overview

Sable Orin is one of the senior Coordinators of The Gray Line, a logistics network operating between Solenne and the Fractured Zones.

Publicly she owns Orin Transit, a legitimate freight company.

Privately she manages smuggling routes, information exchanges, and unofficial operations.


## Personality

Sable is calm, controlled, and extremely observant.

She rarely shows emotion because she believes discipline is survival.

She uses dry humor to disarm people and hide what she is thinking.


## Goals

- Keep The Gray Line operational
- Protect her organization from Solenne interference
- Discover what happened during the Gray Line operation connected to Robert Hit Dev's disappearance


## Secrets

A Gray Line operation transported something connected to Robert Hit Dev the night he vanished.

The records disappeared.

The Coordinator responsible later died under suspicious circumstances.


## Player Relationship

The player begins as a contractor.

Trust must be earned through successful missions.

Eventually Sable reveals her personal investigation and asks the player for help.


## Story Importance

Sable connects the player's economic gameplay to the central Robert Hit Dev mystery.
