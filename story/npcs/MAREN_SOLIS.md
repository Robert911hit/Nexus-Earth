
# Director Maren Solis

## Identity

Name:
Maren Solis

Age:
55

Occupation:
Director of Community Integration

Organization:
Solenne Integrated Systems


## Overview

Maren manages Solenne's relationship with The Fringe and oversees Unregistered policies.

She represents the human face of Solenne's corporate government.


## Personality

Thoughtful.

Professional.

Empathetic.

But capable of ruthless decisions when protecting Solenne.


## Goals

- Maintain Solenne stability
- Prevent conflict between the city and The Fringe
- Discover what Robert Hit Dev left behind


## Secrets

Maren previously worked in a classified Solenne research division connected to Robert Hit Dev's disappearance.

She was not responsible, but she knows the institution was involved.


## Player Relationship

She can become:

- Ally
- Employer
- Political opponent
- Uncertain partner


## Story Importance

Maren represents the internal perspective of Solenne and forces the player to question whether systems can change from within.
