# Ezra Voss

## Identity

Name:
Ezra Voss

Age:
71

Occupation:
Historian / Documentary Journalist

Location:
The Fringe


## Overview

Ezra Voss spent decades documenting Unregistered communities across Nexus Earth.

His archive contains one of the largest unofficial collections of human history outside corporate systems.


## Personality

Warm.

Patient.

Sometimes appears distracted.

Actually extremely precise.

He carries decades of responsibility and regret.


## Secrets

Ezra was the last documented person to speak with Robert Hit Dev before his disappearance.

The conversation was never recorded.

Robert asked him to keep it secret.


## Player Relationship

Ezra does not trust people searching for Robert's legacy.

The player must prove they seek understanding, not power.


## Story Importance

Ezra is the emotional center of the Robert Hit Dev mystery.
