The Fringe

Status

Official Solenne District

Overview

The Fringe is the outermost district of Solenne and the final boundary between the Sovereign City and the surrounding Fractured Zones.

It is one of the few places where Unregistered individuals can exist within city limits.

Officially, the district exists to support trade, logistics, and border management.

Unofficially, it has become a meeting point for independent traders, information brokers, travelers, and those who prefer life outside strict corporate oversight.

Visual Identity

- Patchwork architecture
- Reduced corporate presence
- Mixed lighting systems
- Improvised markets
- Border checkpoints
- Semi-abandoned infrastructure

Population

- Auxiliary workers
- Travelers
- Traders
- Unregistered residents
- Independent contractors

Economy

Primary activities include:

- Informal trade
- Logistics support
- Information exchange
- Repair services
- Transportation

Connection To Robert Hit Dev

The Fringe contains many communities influenced by the ideas of Robert Hit Dev.

Copies of the Unregistered Manifesto continue to circulate throughout the district.

Rumors persist that someone in The Fringe knows the truth about Robert's disappearance.
