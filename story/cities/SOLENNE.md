Solenne

Status

Official Sovereign City

Overview

Solenne is one of the most advanced Sovereign Cities in Nexus Earth.

Known for clean energy, biotechnology, advanced infrastructure, and beautiful urban design, Solenne is often presented as proof that humanity successfully rebuilt after the Unraveling.

Visitors often describe Solenne as a city from the future.

Governing Power

Solenne Integrated Systems

The corporation and the city operate as one unified authority.
