The Gray Line

Status

Official Nexus Earth Faction

Primary Region

The Fringe, Solenne

Overview

The Gray Line operates between the formal systems of the Sovereign Cities and the informal systems of the Fractured Zones.

They specialize in moving goods, people, information, and opportunities across borders that were never meant to be crossed.

To some, they are smugglers.

To others, they are logistics experts.

To most, they are simply the people who can get things done.

Leadership

The Coordinators

A small group of experienced operators who oversee major activities and maintain internal standards.

Core Principles

- Professionalism

Activities

- Information brokering
- Trade facilitation

- Logistics

Relationship To Solenne

Unofficial but tolerated.

Solenne Integrated Systems publicly opposes Gray Line activities while quietly benefiting from certain services they provide.

Relationship To Robert Hit Dev

Several Gray Line traditions originated from networks that existed during Robert Hit Dev's era.

Some members believe clues to his disappearance still move through Gray Line channels.

Player Benefits

- Access to underground markets
- Special contracts
- Travel assistance
- Smuggling routes
- Intelligence networks

Risks

Failure, betrayal, or exposure can result in loss of reputation, loss of contracts, or attention from city authorities.
