# Nexus Earth AI Worker Checklist

Before starting any task:

1. Read the Master Blueprint.

2. Read the AI Work Rules.

3. Read the assigned task file.

4. Understand the goal before creating anything.

5. Follow the approved technology stack.

6. Keep work modular.

7. Explain what was created.

8. Save work to the correct location.

9. Do not change unrelated systems.

10. Report completion clearly.
