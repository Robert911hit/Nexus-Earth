# Nexus Earth

## Vision

A next-generation open-world RPG where players can create their own path.

Players can:

- Own businesses
- Buy houses
- Drive vehicles
- Form organizations
- Complete missions
- Trade with players
- Explore the world
- Build a reputation

## Technology
